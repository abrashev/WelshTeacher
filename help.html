"Welsh Teacher" Help instructions

Use mouse to choose one of the three boxes and answer questions! You can of course use completely just keyboard

  • Enter: to play next question or play again
  • 1: to pick left box
  • 2: to pick center box
  • 3: to pick right box
  • Esc: to end the game
  • N: to change your name
  • H: to see this help box
  • Tip: for each level you have to answer correctly at 75% of the questions otherwize you have to start the level from scratch"
